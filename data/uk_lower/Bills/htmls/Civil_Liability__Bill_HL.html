Latest news on the Civil Liability Act 2018

Following agreement by both Houses on the text of the Bill it received Royal Assent on 20 December 2018. The Bill is now an Act of Parliament (law).

 

Summary of the Civil Liability Act 2018

A Bill to make provision about whiplash claims and the personal injury discount rate.
